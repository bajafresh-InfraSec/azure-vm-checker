Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to azsize.com ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and CLI tool.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • When you use our website or CLI tool, we do not require you to create an account or provide personal information
  • If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and message content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes:
    • Pages visited
    • Time spent on site
    • Browser type and version
    • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
    • Geographic location (country/region level)
    • Referring website
  • Search Queries: When you check VM availability, we log the VM size and region(s) you searched for (anonymously, not linked to your identity)

2.3 Cookies

  • We use Google Analytics cookies to track website usage
  • We use localStorage to save your last search (VM size and regions) for convenience
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Understand how users interact with our website
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

  • You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings
  • You can also opt out by visiting www.aboutads.info

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website analytics (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdSense: For displaying advertisements (Privacy Policy)
  • Azure (Microsoft): For hosting and API services (Privacy Policy)

6. Data Retention

  • Google Analytics data: Retained for 26 months (Google's default)
  • Search query logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Support emails: Retained for 1 year

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking (use browser extensions like uBlock Origin)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (see Section 4)

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: feedback@azsize.com
  • Website: https://www.azsize.com
  • GitHub: https://github.com/bajafresh-InfraSec/azsize/issues

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request disclosure of the personal information we collect, and the right to request deletion of your personal information. Contact us at feedback@azsize.com to exercise these rights.

13. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

Contact us at feedback@azsize.com to exercise these rights.
